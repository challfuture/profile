My Short Story

I decided to challenge who I am and embark on a new career. I wanted something that can summon my creative thinking which will allow me to turn my thoughts to things. So, not knowing where the yellow brick road would lead, my curiosity (which killed the cat) lead me to Front-end development and I never looked back. I enjoy seeing the blends of great photography, beautiful colors, and artistic typography come to life.

I’ve been a professional paralegal with a curious thirst for knowledge and new skills.

Moral of the story, follow your curiosity. The cat survived, with eight more lives which did not look too shabby after all.
